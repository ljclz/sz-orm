Struct InMemoryEsSync

In-memory EsSync implementation backed by a HashMap<index, HashMap<id, EsDocument>>.

Suitable for unit tests and small in-process workloads. search supports MatchAll, Term (exact match), Terms (membership), Range (numeric/string bounds), and Bool (must/should/must_not).
